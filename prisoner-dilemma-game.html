EDUCATIONAL SIGNIFICANCE

The Prisoner's Dilemma is a fundamental concept in game theory that explores cooperation vs. self-interest.

It demonstrates how rational individuals may not cooperate even when it's in their best interest,

and has applications in economics, psychology, politics, and evolutionary biology.

By playing against different strategies, you'll learn how cooperation emerges and evolves.
